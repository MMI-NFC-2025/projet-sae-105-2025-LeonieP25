Explanation of the topic

My topic consists of building a mobile website around the Matilda Effect, a phenomenon that highlights how some women scientists have seen their discoveries attributed to men. On this website, you can discover the journey of Nettie Stevens, the biologist who identified the X and Y sex chromosomes, whose work was long overlooked. The site also presents several complementary articles: women scientists in literature, a popularized explanation of Stevens’ discovery, a film illustrating the invisibility of women, and a book borrowed from the university library related to the topic. A timeline, a film gallery, and a glossary further enrich the navigation. Finally, a contact page allows visitors to get in touch with the site. Some textual elements were written with the help of an AI.
