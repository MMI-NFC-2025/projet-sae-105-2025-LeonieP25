Project explanation

My project is to build a mobile website around the Matilda Effect, a phenomenon that highlights how some women scientists have seen their discoveries attributed to men. On this site, you can discover the story of Nettie Stevens, the biologist who identified the X and Y sex chromosomes, but whose work was long ignored.

The site also features several complementary articles: on women scientists in literature, a popularization of Stevens' discovery, a film illustrating the invisibility of women, and a book borrowed from the university library related to the topic. A timeline, a film gallery, and a glossary enrich the navigation. Finally, a contact page allows you to get in touch with the site. Some textual elements were written with the help of AI.
